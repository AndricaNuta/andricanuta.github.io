Generate Favicon with Rounded Corners

This tool loads your SVG favicon and generates PNG/ICO files with rounded corners.

Instructions:
  1. Open this file in your browser (it should load the SVG from ../public/favicon.svg)
  2. Wait for the preview to load
  3. Click the buttons to download the favicon files
  4. Replace the files in the public/ folder with the downloaded files
  5. Important: Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete or Cmd+Shift+Delete) and hard refresh (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R) to see the new favicon
  6. For ICO files, you can rename the 32x32 PNG to .ico, or use an online converter
