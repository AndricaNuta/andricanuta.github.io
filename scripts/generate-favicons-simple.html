Generate Favicon with Rounded Corners

This tool will generate PNG and ICO favicon files with rounded corners.

Instructions:
  1. Click the buttons above to download the favicon files
  2. Save "favicon.png" to the public/ folder
  3. For ICO files, you may need to use an online converter or tool like ImageMagick
  4. Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete or Cmd+Shift+Delete) to see the new favicon
